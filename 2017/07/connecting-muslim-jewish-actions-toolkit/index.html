Connecting Actions 2021 - 5th Symposium on Dialogue

On November 15, 16 and 17 2021, Connecting Actions convened the 5th symposium of experts and leaders in the field of dialogue.

Over 20 Civil Society Leaders in the field of Dialogue gathered online for 3 days of rich discussions. They brainstormed common objectives and analysed common challenges. They shared skills and knowledge about their respective practices. Committed to reinforcing this field, Connecting Actions will continue to promote training, collaboration and joint initiatives for a bigger impact.

Join us if you want to learn more about citizen, interconvictional and intercultural dialogue and contribute to its development!

Dialogue in Youth Work.

On January 2017 Members of the Connecting Actions Network gathered with the support of the European Youth Foundation and the Council of Europe to design this toolkit, specifically adapted to Muslim Jewish Dialogue activities. Need ideas for your Muslim-Jewish peace work? Please help yourself!
