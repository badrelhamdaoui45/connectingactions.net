]]> Middle East In Dialogue Report https://connectingactions.net/2023/06/middle-east-in-dialogue-report/ Tue, 20 Jun 2023 16:49:10 +0000 https://connectingactions.net/?p=269 Read more about Middle East In Dialogue Report[...]]]> Report “Middle East in Dialogue”

In July 2022, Connecting Actions joined forces with the Alliance for Middle East Peace (ALLMEP) to initiate and lead a Symposium of civil society leaders to discuss and learn about the art of facilitating difficult conversations in Europe around the theme of Israel-Palestine. The goal was to equip those participants with knowledge, skills and tools to facilitate constructive dialogue over this difficult topic, overcoming both unhealthy avoidance and destructive clashes. The Report of this conference is available in French and in English.

E-book: “Dialogue, Not Hate – Best practices for Citizen Dialogue”.
